# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule: **be kind, be constructive, assume good intent.**

## Expected behavior

- Treat requestors, reviewers, SMEs, and mentors with respect.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Credit people whose work you build on.
- Ask questions instead of making assumptions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Dismissing requests or feedback because of who filed them.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
(`@tdevere`) directly via a GitHub private mention or email associated with
their GitHub account. Reports are handled confidentially.

## Consequences

The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, PRs, and other
contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or
permanently ban contributors whose behavior is deemed inappropriate.
